Brandon’s Health journey began in 2012 when he was diagnosed with over 5 different “incurable” health disorders during his College Career as a former Division 1 Athlete. Most notably, he was suffering from Anxiety/Panic Attack Disorder, Eczema/Psoriasis, Bouts of Depression, Hypertension, and Sleep Apnea, among others. After listening to the “best” that western medical doctors could advise (nothing but several different prescription medications and blaming “genetics” for the apparently “lifelong” ailments,) he decided to take his health into his own hands.

Within 3-6 months of committing himself to learning about Biology, Physiology, the Nervous System, as well as ancient medical practices such as Ayurveda and Chinese Medicine, he was able to lose 35+pounds of fat, completely solve his eczema and sleep apnea, drastically reduce his anxiety and solve his depression. To this day, after 8+ years of studying and practicing as a Certified Performance Coach, High Tech Wellness Consultant, and Lead Health Strategist for some of the top Regenerative Clinics in the U.S., he still works 1 on 1 with clients to help provide strategies and solutions for chronic and debilitating health issues in the U.S.
