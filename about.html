Brandon is a certified Health and Performance Coach and Lead Health Strategy Advisor for several top Regenerative Wellness Clinics throughout the U.S. In 2012 Brandon suffered from a debilitating knee surgery that left him with rampant and sudden health disorders, seemingly “incurable” by western medical standards. This led him to find real solutions and “cures” to his health issues by studying nutrition, exercise/movement, Ayurvedic medicine, and regenerative/functional medicine and “Biohacking.”

After transforming his life through his health, Brandon has went on to personally coach hundreds if not thousands of clients through inner and outer transformation, and now utilizes his other areas of expertise including Astrology, Intuitive Tarot Guidance, and Energy Healing to work with individuals to drastically change the direction of their life and help them find inner clarity, purpose, and true happiness/fulfillment in ways they could only imagine before.

Brandon is also passionate about motivational speaking, holistic health and spiritual education, and sharing guidance on his Youtube Channel and Podcast, Science of the Soul and Stars. His dream is to travel the World impacting the lives of millions while speaking publicly and hosting educational seminars about:

  • Astrology
  • Spirituality
  • Health
  • Finding Passion and Purpose
