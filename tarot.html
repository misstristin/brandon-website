Brandon’s journey with the Tarot began a few years after studying Astrology. After working with client s exclusively around their Natal Birth Chart, one day he was introduced to the “Wild Unknown” Tarot Deck from a good friend, who allowed him to do some “practice readings” for her for fun.

After realizing he was “spot on” about his friend’s situation despite not knowing anything about it, and relying solely on Tarot, he knew he had to learn more about this special practice. He soon began studying and mastering the identities of the cards for himself. Now he utilizes it as a necessary adjunct in sessions for clients to provide any immediate guidance or insight around particular situations in love and in life.

Tarot Session

In a Tarot Session with Brandon, you will get:

  • A 45 minute recorded video going in-depth into your situation
  • Session can be a General Reading on Love or on Life, OR around up to 3 specific questions you’re facing
  • Deeper Insights into a situation occurring in your life and the lessons you’re learning through it
  • Current energies involved in your life and advice on how best to navigate them
  • Intuitive/Channeled Messages from your Guides-”What they need you to hear now!”

Whether you’re looking for clarity in a particular situation or General Guidance from your Spirit Guides, I believe Tarot is one of the most useful and direct tools we have to clarify and receive life-changing insight into your current life.
