Through his journey, Brandon has credited much of his growth, transformation, and progress as a result of several dedicated mentors and coaches who helped to guide him where he wanted to go. To help aspiring Lightworkers step into their true Spiritual gifts and learn to use them for the World, both for themselves and for other people, just as Brandon has and continues to do through his own journey.

Transformational Guidance Program

In this program, you will get:

  • Personalized, 1 on 1 guidance and meetings on a weekly basis
  • An ongoing Health Optimization Plan, strategies, and advice to optimize their physical wellbeing
  • Spiritual Guidance and healing as necessary-for this we use Astrology, Tarot, and Energy Healing to get your life on track
  • Deep dive into your “why” for life and really mastering what you’re here to do and be for the World so you can feel fulfilled and progress with your true life path
  • Regular Access to Brandon via email or text for fast Spiritual Guidance when needed
  • Physical, Mental, Emotional, and Spiritual Healing/Optimization to ensure that you are in full alignment with the greatest potentials of your life
  • I will hold you accountable to the goals we set during sessions!
